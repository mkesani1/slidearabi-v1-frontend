Layout Accuracy

Arabic layout isn't just translation.
It's reconstruction.

Every element in a slide changes when you go from English to Arabic. Here's what our AI handles automatically.

Text Direction (RTL)

Arabic reads right-to-left. Every text box, bullet list, and paragraph must reverse. Alignment, indentation, and reading order all flip — including nested content.

Layout Mirroring

Everything spatial flips. Images that were on the left move right. Charts, tables, and diagrams reposition. Margins and padding mirror. It's a full spatial reconstruction.

Typography

Arabic glyphs are taller and connect in ways Latin characters don't. Font sizes, line heights, and spacing must be recalculated for every text box to look professional.
