Arabic layout isn't just translation.
It's reconstruction.

Every element in a slide changes when you go from English to Arabic. Here's what our AI handles automatically.

Text Direction (RTL)

Arabic reads right-to-left. Every text box, bullet list, and paragraph must reverse. Alignment, indentation, and reading order all flip — including nested content.

Layout Mirroring

Everything spatial flips. Images that were on the left move right. Charts, tables, and diagrams reposition. Margins and padding mirror. It's a full spatial reconstruction.

Typography

Arabic glyphs are taller and connect in ways Latin characters don't. Font sizes, line heights, and spacing must be recalculated for every text box to look professional.

How Our AI Works

Four specialized AI agents work together to convert your presentation with precision.

Translation

Context-aware Arabic translation that preserves business terminology, brand names, and technical jargon. Not just word-for-word — it understands meaning.

Layout Conversion

Full spatial mirroring of every element. Text boxes, images, shapes, and groupings reposition from LTR to RTL while maintaining visual balance.

Charts & Data

Bar charts, pie charts, and tables are rebuilt with RTL axis orientation. Data labels translate while numerical data stays accurate.

Quality Assurance

Final pass checks for text overflow, alignment consistency, font rendering, and visual balance. Catches issues before you see the preview.
